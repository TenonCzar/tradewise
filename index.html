What Are CryptoCurrencies

Cryptocurrencies are digital or virtual currencies that use cryptography for security. Unlike traditional currencies issued by governments (like the US dollar or the Euro), cryptocurrencies operate on decentralized systems using blockchain technology.

Understanding Chart Patterns

Chart patterns are shapes that form on a price chart of an asset, like a stock or cryptocurrency. These patterns can help traders predict future price movements based on past behavior..

What Is Forex
